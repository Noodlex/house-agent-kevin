# House Agent Kevin 🏠🕵️

Simulate presence in your home while you're away — *Home Alone* style.

Arm one master switch before leaving for holiday and Kevin makes the house *live*: interior lights turn on and off in a plausible, never-repeating order each evening, anchored to the real sunset. Comes with a Lovelace timeline card that shows exactly what will happen, day by day, over your holiday dates — superimposed on the sun's day/night curve.
